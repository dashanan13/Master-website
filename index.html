About Us

Our mission is to empower the society to reduce the food waste in Norway.

The way people search for food has changed. We work to improve public health, cut emissions and waste in the food sector of Oslo, by connecting people to access healthy and sustainable food.

Our vision is to create a food pick-up platform that is easily accessible to everyone.

In everything we do, we believe our customers will be more successful and save a huge amount of expenses if we follow these principles:

  • Be Self-driven: the local people, nearby restaurants or organizations should contribute and donate their redundant food.
  • Be Accessible: we believe that everyone can have access to healthy food at an affordable cost.
  • Be Human: we set a high prioritization of the sustainability for the future generations by utilizing renewable energy for our solutions.
‘The future in our hands’. Better for you and better for the planet.
